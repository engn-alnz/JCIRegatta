Balangaw

'Balangaw' is one of our flagship programs, advocating for the Awareness, Acceptability, and Accessibility of Persons with Special Needs (PSNs) since 2011. Through this, our PSNs have learned to embrace their uniqueness despite their differences, striving for normalcy, and building a spectrum that shines.
