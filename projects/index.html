Be a Member

There is a limit to what governments are able to achieve in society. As responsible citizens in a globalized world, JCI Regatta members take on the challenges around them through local development initiatives.

  • TOSIA

    TOSIA (The Outstanding Students of Iloilo Awards) is an annual Youth Development Program of JCI Regatta that gives due honor and credit to Ilonggo students who excel in four pillars namely: academics, leadership excellence, community involvement, and faith.

  • Balangaw
    For People with Special Needs

    BALANGAW is JCI Regatta's flagship program that advocates for Awareness, Acceptability, and Accessibility of Persons with Special Needs (PSNs)

  • Duag kag Kasanag
    Christmas Gifting-giving Activity

    “DUAG KAG KASANAG”, a Christmas Gift Giving Activity seeks to fulfill the wishes and dreams of those in need, especially the underserved community.

  • Notebook mo, Recycle mo
    Notebook Recycling Project

    JCI Regatta innovated a project entitled “Notebook mo, Recycle ko ”wherein unused pages of notebooks are gathered and put together to make new notebooks. Through this, we can save more trees and take part in mitigating climate change. The products of this project were distributed to our partner schools and beneficiaries.

  • Panublion
    Community Project for Indigenous People

    Panublion, a Hiligaynon word for “legacy”, is a local cultural affairs program of JCI Regatta that aims to alleviate the poverty issues of the Aeta community in Panay and Guimaras Island.

  • Project UGAT

    JCI Regatta did not just initiate a program, we initiated a movement. Project UGAT is a movement to go beyond just planting, we need to go even deeper to go even further.

  • Project Kaakbay
    For Mental Health Awareness

    “Project Kaakbay”, a mental health initiative for the Ilonggo teachers that encourages them to be mindful and maintain their mental well-being. endeavors to help teachers who work in any public or private pre-school, elementary, or high school in Iloilo City.

  • S.H.E.
    Self Health Educational Program for National Women's Month
  • Project ABKD+
    Startup Educational Project
  • REACH
    Digital Marketing Campaign

    The REACH Campaign (Respond, Empower, Access, Connect, and Help) showcases our commitment to adapt and innovate in today's digital world.This digital marketing campaign have strengthened partnerships and volunteerism, linking us with local communities and forging new global alliances.
