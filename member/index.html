10 Things to Know
About JCI

01

Leadership Development

At JCI our members get access to hands-on leadership experiences at the local, national and international level

  • Become a more confident and capable leader
  • Rank up your soft skills and become a more marketable leader
02

Grow Your Professional Network

Access a world-wide network of enterprising young leaders age 18-40, spanning all industries, to grow your professional reach

  • Lead generation through an international network
  • Access a unique online community of like-minded enterprising people
  • Resources and opportunities from partners such as BNI, WBAF
03

Attend Exciting Global Events

Enrich yourself with cultural experiences and skills development opportunities at our five annual conferences held in stunning locations like Curacao, Sakai Takashi, Bruges, Hammamet, and more!

04

Get Involved, Be Innovative

Active Citizens use the action framework as our roadmap to create sustainable impact and to create positive change in our community and ourselves.

  • Analyze
  • Develop
  • Execute
  • Review
05

Special Access to JCI Experts

From opportunities to develop business pitching skills to courses and seminars on Emotional Intelligence, Financial Management and Entrepreneurship, we create opportunities for members to engage with experts across many disciplines

06

Grow Your Social Network

Break the stagnant trends of the digital age and make in-person connections - add driven and ethical leaders to your social circle

  • Expand your personal network through meaningful connections
  • Join our global family of over 200,000 alumni and members from over 100 countries
  • Make lifelong friends from all over the world
07

Learn New Skills

We make sure to keep our Skills Development materials and offers up to date and in accordance to a fast paced world. Our Library contains several different courses for our members that are available at all times and in constant updating according world trends and our members feedback.

08

Boost Your CV

ever hear “we’re looking for someone with more experience” ever again. With JCI, you will run out of space while listing your accomplishments

  • Gain unique experience and connections that will help you get ahead
  • Trackable and hands-on growth and life experience
09

Join the Next Generation of Leaders

JCI is an organization with a 100-year legacy. We're planning a hundred more.

10

Push Yourself Hard to Succeed

JCI asks for the best from its members, and gives them the best opportunities, experiences and support in return. If you are ready to give it your all and see that investment returned to you, JCI is where you should be.
